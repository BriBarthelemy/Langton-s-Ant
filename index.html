Langton's Ant JS

... is a two-dimensional Turing machine with a very simple set of rules but complex emergent behavior. [Wikipedia]
Rules are: when the Ant () is on white tile, it goes right, if black it goes left. When it leaves the tile, the tile toggles to the other color.
Note: the "ant" will stop when it goes out of grid.
Speed : The ant moves every 100 milliseconds.
